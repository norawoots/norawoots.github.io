Hi! I'm Nora.

I'm currently a junior at Carleton College pursuring degrees in Mathematics and Computer Science and considering a potential minor in Spanish. On campus, I work in the Mathematics Department as a grader for MATH 232 (Linear Algebra) and as a PEAR Lifeguard. I am also a member of Carleton's NCAA DIII Varsity Swim Team where I compete in Butterfly and Mid-Distance Freestyle.
Outside of school, I have been working as a Cabin Couselor and Section Head at Pok-O-MacCready Summer Camps in the Adirondacks of upstate New York since June 2023. This past summer, I worked closely with our Summer Camp Director to develop and run our Counselor-In-Training program for 15-16-year-old campers as CIT Section Head. I plan on returning to the same role this coming summer to continue to build cooperative and interpersonal soft skills and to grow the program, a mechanism vital to Poko counselor retention and qualification. At Poko, besides running CIT, I teach swim classes as a Red Cross Lifeguard, lead overnight and day wilderness trips as a WFA-Certified Trip Leader, and mentor children ages 6 to 16. Feel free to read more about the benefits of working at a summer camp.
In my free time I love reading, playing guitar, listening to music, and backpacking and hiking in the Adirondacks. Inspired by this passion, I began designing t-shirts featuring original mountain art specific to the 46 ADK Peaks with elevations above 4000ft, also called 46ers. I launched my Etsy site and business, 46Silhouettes, in August 2023 and since then I have been working with an on-demand print company to fulfill orders quickly and sustainably.
Please enjoy exploring my website more!
