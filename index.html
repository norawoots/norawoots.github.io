Hi! I'm Nora.

I'm currently a junior at Carleton College pursuring degrees in Mathematics and Computer Science and considering a potential minor in Spanish. On campus, I work in the Mathematics Department as a grader for MATH 232 (Linear Algebra) and as a PEAR Lifeguard. I am also a member of Carleton's NCAA DIII Varsity Swim Team where I compete in Butterfly and Mid-Distance Freestyle.
Outside of school, I have been working as a Cabin Couselor and Section Head at Pok-O-MacCready Summer Camps in the Adirondacks of upstate New York since June of 2023. This past summer, I worked closely with our Summer Camp Director to develop and run our Counselor-In-Training (CIT) program for 15-16 year old campers as CIT Section Head. I plan on returning to this role this coming summer to continue to build cooperative and interpersonal soft skills and to grow the program, a mechanism vital to Poko counselor retention and qualification. At Poko, besides running CIT, I teach swim classes as a RED-Cross Lifeguard, lead overnight and day wilderness trips as a WFA-Certified Trip Leader, and mentor children ages 6 to 16.
